The Situation:
I was hired as a data analyst by the newly elected franchise owner 'Alex' by Maven Rosters, a coffee shop chain with 3 Locations in New York City

The Assignment:
To better understand the purchase history and streamline operations, Alex has provided me the Transaction data from Jan to Jun 2023.
My goal is to transform the data into dynamic dashboard that franchise owner can use to identify patterns, trends and opportunities for the business.

The Objectives:
1. Profile and Prepare the raw data for analysis
2. Explore the data with Excel Pivot tables
3. Build a dynamic dashboard to visualize patterns and trends
